# Expense Claims Guide

## What You Can Claim

- **Travel** for business purposes — flights, trains, taxis to and
  from the airport, ride-shares between meetings.
- **Accommodation** at the rate band approved for the destination
  city. The People Portal lists the bands; ask the People team if the
  destination is missing.
- **Meals while travelling** up to a daily cap of $80 (breakfast,
  lunch, dinner combined). Alcohol is not reimbursable except as part
  of a pre-approved client dinner.
- **Client entertainment** with manager pre-approval and a clear
  business purpose recorded in the claim notes.
- **Conference fees** when the conference is approved as part of your
  development plan.
- **Office supplies** for remote workers up to $200 per year, against
  a receipt.

## What You Cannot Claim

- Personal travel or accommodation upgrades beyond the approved band.
- Personal entertainment (films, gym, spa) even while travelling.
- Gifts for individual customers above $50 in value (corporate gifts
  go through Marketing, not Expenses).
- Items purchased for personal use that "happen to also be useful for
  work" without prior manager approval.

## How to Submit

1. Log in to the Expenses portal.
2. Create a new claim with a short, descriptive title (e.g.
   "Customer X visit, 12-14 March").
3. Attach a clear photo or scan of every receipt. The portal accepts
   PDF, PNG, or JPG.
4. Categorise each line correctly (Travel / Accommodation / Meals /
   etc.) — incorrect categorisation slows the approval.
5. Submit. Your line manager will be notified automatically.

## Approval and Reimbursement

- Manager approval target: **3 working days**.
- Finance processing target: **5 working days** after manager
  approval.
- Reimbursement is paid to the bank account on file in the People
  Portal. Update your bank details there before submitting if they
  have changed.

## Receipts and Records

Keep original receipts for 90 days after submission. Finance may
sample-audit claims and request the original. Without the original,
the claim may be reversed.

## Currency

Submit claims in the original currency of the receipt. The Expenses
portal converts to your payroll currency at the prevailing exchange
rate on the date of the transaction. Do not pre-convert manually.
