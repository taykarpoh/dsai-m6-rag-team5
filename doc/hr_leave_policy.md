# HR Leave Policy

## Annual Leave

All full-time employees accrue 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar
year. Leave accrues at the rate of 1.67 days per completed month of
service. Up to 5 days of unused annual leave may be carried into the
following year; any balance above 5 days is forfeited on 31 December.

Contractors and part-time staff do not accrue annual leave under this
policy. Their leave entitlement is governed by their individual contract
of engagement.

## Requesting Leave

Submit leave requests through the People Portal at least 10 working days
in advance for any period longer than 2 days. Shorter requests should
still be made before the leave is taken, except for emergency leave.

Approval is at the discretion of the line manager and is subject to
operational requirements. The People team will mediate where a manager
declines a request without a clear reason.

## Sick Leave

Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per calendar year.
Absences of 3 or more consecutive working days require a medical
certificate. Persistent short-term absences may trigger a wellbeing
conversation with the People team; this is supportive rather than
punitive.

## Parental Leave

Primary caregivers are entitled to 16 weeks of paid parental leave;
secondary caregivers to 4 weeks. Both can be taken in a single block or
split across the first 12 months after birth or placement. Notify the
People team at least 8 weeks before the planned start date.

## Carer's Leave

Up to 5 days per year may be taken to care for an immediate family
member who is ill or injured. Carer's leave draws from the sick leave
balance.

## Public Holidays

Public holidays are paid days off and do not count against any leave
balance. The list of recognised holidays is published in January each
year.
